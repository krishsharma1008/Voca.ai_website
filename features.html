VOCA AssistIQ

Real-Time Intelligence

AssistIQ listens to every live call and delivers contextual nudges the moment they matter — not after the conversation is over.

Live Intent Scoring

Every utterance is scored for purchase intent, upsell readiness, and churn risk in real time, giving agents a live signal map of where the conversation stands.

Objection Detection

Common objection patterns are identified the moment they surface. Agents receive real-time response prompts drawn from top-performer playbooks.

Next-Best-Action Nudges

Context-aware suggestions for upsell, cross-sell, offer framing, and escalation delivered directly to the agent's screen without interrupting the call.

Sentiment Analysis

Continuous tone and sentiment tracking surfaces frustration, hesitation, and enthusiasm in real time so agents can modulate and managers can intervene.

Near-Miss Recovery

When a high-intent call drops without conversion, AssistIQ automatically triggers prioritised callback queues, SMS follow-ups, and email reachout flows.

Dead-Air & Silence Detection

Prolonged silences trigger instant coaching prompts to help agents re-engage prospects and avoid call drops due to awkward pauses.

VOCA VoiceOS

Voice Autonomy

VoiceOS is a production-grade autonomous voice stack that handles full inbound and outbound conversations across 23 Indic languages and English.

Multilingual IVR

Replace rigid DTMF trees with natural-language IVR that understands and responds across Hindi, Tamil, Telugu, Kannada, Bengali, Marathi, and 17 more Indic languages plus English.

Human-Like Voice Synthesis

Low-latency neural TTS produces natural prosody, appropriate pausing, and human-style turn-taking so callers cannot distinguish the AI from a live agent.

Accent Coverage

ASR models fine-tuned on regional accent variations ensure accurate speech recognition regardless of dialect, code-switching, or mixed-language utterances.

Intelligent Call Routing

Intent and language signals dynamically route calls to the right queue, agent skill-set, or VoiceOS flow — eliminating manual dispatch and misrouting delays.

Controlled Human Handoff

Confidence-gated escalation hands complex or high-value interactions to live agents with full context transfer — no caller repetition, no context loss.

Inbound & Outbound Flows

VoiceOS manages both inbound resolution and outbound dialer campaigns — from collections and renewals to lead qualification and appointment setting.

Post-Call Summaries

Rep Scorecards

Individual performance profiles track nudge acceptance rate, objection handling score, conversion by call type, and talk-to-listen ratio over time.

Searchable Call Transcripts

Every call is transcribed, tagged by topic, and indexed for full-text search — enabling QA teams to surface specific moments in seconds.

Enterprise Trust

Compliance & Security

VOCA is built for regulated industries. Every deployment ships with the controls, consent mechanisms, and audit infrastructure enterprise teams require.

PCI-DSS Ready

Payment card data is masked and redacted from call recordings and transcripts. DTMF capture for card entry never traverses VOCA systems in clear text.

GDPR-Ready

Data residency controls, right-to-erasure workflows, and configurable retention policies support GDPR and DPDP Act compliance requirements.

Call Recording Consent

Automated consent beeps, pre-call disclosures, and opt-out handling are configurable per jurisdiction to keep every call legally compliant.

Immutable Audit Logs

Every system action — nudge delivery, escalation trigger, CRM write — is logged with a tamper-evident timestamp for regulatory review and QA.

Role-Based Access Control

Granular RBAC ensures agents, team leads, compliance officers, and admins each see only the data and controls appropriate for their role.

Compliance Guardrails

Script compliance checks flag when agents deviate from required disclosures or regulated language, with real-time alerts and post-call QA flagging.
